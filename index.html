How to secure sponsorship

Have experience

Companies want to ensure that your organisation is committed to its vision and mission.

Find corporates that align with your values

It is easier to reach a common goal with a company that aligns with your organisations values, beliefs, mission, and vision.

Offer something valuable

Corporate sponsors often want a return on their investment. Consider how you can work together to create an outcome that benefits both of you.

Follow up

It is important to keep in mind that corporates are often very busy running their companies. You will need to keep in touch.
